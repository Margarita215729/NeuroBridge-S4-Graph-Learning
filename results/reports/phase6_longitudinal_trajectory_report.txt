Limitations
----------------------------------------------------------------
  - Self-baseline analysis requires longitudinal data across mission phases.
  - Schema demonstration data test the pipeline only; they are not evidence.
  - Hazard-context deltas are interpretation context, not exposure measurement.
  - n is very small; individualized trajectories avoid underpowered group inference.

Why self-baseline matters
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Healthy population ranges are insufficient for astronaut monitoring because operationally meaningful changes may occur within population-normal ranges, while stable individual baselines may differ from generic norms.
  Reference cohorts remain useful for calibration but are not the main endpoint.

Interpretation guardrail
----------------------------------------------------------------
  This report describes within-subject graph changes. It is not diagnosis,
  treatment guidance, exposure measurement, or health outcome prediction.
